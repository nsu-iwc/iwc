Part of the Alvin Sherman Library, the Miniaci Performing Arts Center hosts many university events, such as the Distinguished Speakers Series and award ceremonies. The theatre, which is partly managed by the Broward Center for the Performing Arts, is a convenient venue for students and the community to attend cultural programming.
