The Flight Deck Pub is the spot to hang out. Grab your favorite booth or barstool for a pub-style meal, play games, watch TV, or enjoy a cold one with friends on the outside deck. Tucked away in a corner of the University Center, the Flight Deck is a modern, yet cozy restaurant with indoor and outdoor bars, 17 wide-screen TVs, outdoor cabanas with Wi-Fi that each fit 8 to 10 friends, plush lounge furniture, a multipurpose space, and a tranquil plaza. This is a great place to gather, socialize, and relax between and after class.

At the heart of NSU's campus is our 366,000-square-foot Don Taft University Center, a hub of student life. Enter the building's "spine" and you can't help but marvel at the 3-story high undersea mural by renowned marine artist and scientist Guy Harvey. It spans the length of a football field! Cheer on the NCAA Division II Sharks basketball and volleyball teams in The Rick Case Arena, which also hosts community events including concerts, comedians, banquets, and guest speakers. Be inspired by outstanding student and faculty theatre, music, dance, visual art, and other cultural productions held in our Performing and Visual Arts Wing.
