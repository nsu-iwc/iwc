AVAILABLE AUDIO
Not to be confused with our residence hall of the same name, the Leo Goodwin Sr. Hall is the home of the Shepard Broad College of Law. This is where our law students attend classes, hold mock trials in our two model courtrooms, and grab a cup of coffee or a bite to eat at the Supreme Court Cafe. Other College of Law offices including Admissions, Financial Aid, Dean's Office, and Career Development are available on the first floor. The second floor is home to the college's expansive Panza Maurer Law Library and faculty offices.
