AVAILABLE AUDIO
At the heart of NSU's campus is our 366,000-square-foot Don Taft University Center, a hub of student life. Enter the building's "spine" and you can't help but marvel at the 3-story high undersea mural by renowned marine artist and scientist Guy Harvey. It spans the length of a football field! Cheer on the NCAA Division II Sharks basketball and volleyball teams in The Arena, which also hosts community events including concerts, comedians, banquets, and guest speakers. Be inspired by outstanding student and faculty theatre, music, dance, visual art, and other cultural productions held in our Performing and Visual Arts Wing. The University Center's Food Court is a great place to grab a fresh and tasty meal, and you can relax or study at your very own campus Starbucks.
