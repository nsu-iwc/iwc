Since joining the NCAA and the Sunshine State Conference in 2002, NSU Athletics has grown into a highly competitive intercollegiate athletic program with 17 NCAA Division II sports. NSU is proud of our success over the past 10 years and is home to 8 national championship teams, 16 individual national champions, and 27 SSC champions. The program also boasts having over 350 combined NCAA All-Americans and Scholar All-Americans. For 14 consecutive years, NSU student-athletes have averaged greater than a 3.0 GPA, staying competitive in the classroom as well as on the field. Remaining true to our core value of "community," NSU student-athletes have partnered with numerous community affiliates to serve those in need.

RecPlex Pool
MEDIA TYPE
360 Panorama
MEDIA SOURCE
https://www.youvisit.com/media/94219/panoramas/114587/1024.jpg
AUDIO GUIDE
We're proud to be strong in both mind and body. So, work hard then play hard at our RecPlex, a state-of-the-art wellness and fitness center. Make a splash with aquatics classes. Compete in intramural sports. Learn a new hobby with instructional programs like scuba or golf. The modern facilities include indoor cardiovascular and strength training equipment, two-court multipurpose gym, racquetball courts, and a 2-story rock climbing wall, as well as group exercise classes including spinning, yoga, Zumba, Hip Hop, and much more. Or you can just relax at the sparkling outdoor pool and enjoy the South Florida sun. How you spend your time at the RecPlex is up to you, but we're sure we'll see you there.
