Rosenthal Student Center Audio:
The Rosenthal Student Center is home to several offices which serve the NSU student community. Located on the eastside of the Don Taft University Center near the Shark Fountain, Rosenthal offers meeting space for numerous clubs and organizations. Stop by the first floor to make a request at NSU's student radio station, Radio X or get assistance from the Office of Student Disability Services. Head upstairs to find open and contemporary meeting spaces for NSU's clubs and organizations and the Veteran's Resource Center.

AUDIO GUIDE
Part of the Alvin Sherman Library, the Miniaci Performing Arts Center hosts many university events, such as the Distinguished Speakers Series and award ceremonies. The theater, which is partly managed by the Broward Center for the Performing Arts, is a convenient venue for students and the community to attend cultural programming.

NSU Art Museum
MEDIA TYPE
360 Panorama
MEDIA SOURCE
https://www.youvisit.com/media/94219/panoramas/114595/1024.jpg
AUDIO GUIDE
NSU Art Museum is a modern 83,000-square-foot building located in the heart of downtown Fort Lauderdale. The museum is home to a permanent collection of more than 6,000 works in 25,000 square feet of exhibition space. NSU Art Museum also includes a 256-seat auditorium, museum store, and cafe. All NSU students, faculty, and staff gain free entrance to the museum's enriching programming, while students in the art and arts administration majors may consider the museum their second home with its opportunities for internships. The museum also hosts many of our faculty lectures.
