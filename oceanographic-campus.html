AVIALABLE AUDIO
NSU's Oceanographic Campus occupies prime South Florida real estate at the entrance to Port Everglades with immediate access to the Atlantic Ocean. The campus is home to five buildings including the state-of-the-art NSU Guy Harvey Oceanographic Center, an 86,000-square-foot Silver LEED Certified structure that contains laboratories, offices, seminar rooms, an 85-seat auditorium, and indoor and outdoor running seawater facilities. The Oceanographic Center's design supports impactful research by faculty, researchers, visiting scientists, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students in various areas including coral reefs, genomics, sharks, fisheries, and biology.
