Living on campus means you're never far from home. And it's the place where you'll create memories and friendships that last a lifetime. This modern co-ed living community includes residence hall suites, community living rooms, study rooms, and plenty of indoor and outdoor common space. Suite amenities include private bathrooms, Wi-Fi, cable TV, and in-room phones. Our themed housing options give you the choice to live in the Quiet Corridor, Leadership Floor, or the Green Village. The Commons courtyard is an outdoor space with a volleyball court and picnic area. Check out the videos to get a sense of campus life in sunny South Florida.
